Chicken with Artichokes and Mushrooms

Description

This fried chicken-tasting mushroom is called Laetiporus sulphureus and is more commonly known as 'Chicken of the Woods. ' It can be found across Europe and North America, mainly on oak trees, but can also be found on yew, cherry, and sweet chestnut trees.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Olive oil and butter
  • Mushrooms
  • Italian seasoning
  • Garlic
  • Chicken broth
  • Lemon juice
  • Dijon mustard
  • Heavy cream

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken breasts in half lengthwise, then season and coat the cutlets in flour and sear on both sides in a skillet until golden.
  2. Transfer to a plate, and then saut√© the mushrooms.
  3. Take the mushrooms out of the pan and add the garlic, chicken broth, lemon juice, and Dijon mustard.
  4. Let it reduce, and then stir in the cream along with the mushrooms and chicken.
  5. Finish cooking the chicken through, and season with salt & pepper.
